Frequently Asked Questions

How does Meridian find connections between my signals?

Meridian uses statistical correlation analysis on your daily logs. After 7 days of data, the app calculates relationships between your signals (e.g., how sleep affects your energy). All analysis runs locally on your device.

Is my health data sent to any servers?

No. All data stays on your device. Meridian has no servers, no accounts, and no cloud sync. Your data is yours alone.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Data > Export Data (JSON). This creates a file containing all your logs and settings that you can save or share.

Can I add custom signals?

Custom signals are coming in a future update. Currently, Meridian supports 8 signals: sleep, energy, mood, focus, stress, headache, exercise, and digestion.

What do the colored lines on the network graph mean?

Blue/teal lines indicate positive correlations (when one signal goes up, the other tends to go up). Red/warm lines indicate negative correlations (when one goes up, the other tends to go down). Thicker lines mean stronger connections.

Is Meridian medical advice?

No. Meridian is an informational tool that helps you notice patterns in your daily health signals. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. All insights are backed by published research citations. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical decisions.

How do I reset my data?

Go to Settings > Data > Reset All Data. This permanently deletes all logs, settings, and insights. This action cannot be undone.
