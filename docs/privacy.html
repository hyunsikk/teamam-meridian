Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Overview

Meridian: Health Insights ("Meridian", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

Meridian collects and stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically:

  • Health signals: Sleep, energy, mood, focus, stress, headache, exercise, and digestion data you log is stored only on your device using local storage.
  • Settings and preferences: Your theme preference, active signals, and notification settings are stored locally.
  • No accounts: Meridian does not require account creation, login, or any personal identifying information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising networks
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or photos

Local Storage

All health data is stored using AsyncStorage on your device. This data persists between app sessions but does not leave your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the notification is scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Data Export

You can export your data as a JSON file from the Settings screen. This export is handled entirely on your device. You control where the exported file is saved or shared.

Children's Privacy

Meridian is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at teamam.dev@gmail.com.
